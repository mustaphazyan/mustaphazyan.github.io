About Mustapha Zyan

Mustapha Zyan is a contemporary Rai artist who masterfully blends classical Moroccan traditions with modern musical elements. His unique sound has captivated audiences across the globe.

Through his multiple albums, Mustapha offers a distinctive musical experience that reflects the richness of Eastern culture while embracing modern production techniques.
